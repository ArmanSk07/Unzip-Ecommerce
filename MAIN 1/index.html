About Me

Born and bred in the vibrant streets of Mumbai, I’m the founder and creative force behind ArzCollection. My journey began with a simple love for bespoke cricket-inspired apparel—transforming iconic moments into wearable art. Over the years, I’ve blended tradition with innovation, using digital printing techniques and eco-friendly inks to deliver bold, statement-making T-shirts, jackets, and umbrellas that stand the test of time. My design philosophy revolves around authenticity, quality, and the power of self-expression. When you choose ArzCollection, you’re not just wearing fashion—you’re carrying a story. From intimate custom orders to limited-run collections, I work closely with each client to ensure that every piece reflects their personality and passion. Come explore my world of futurism-meets-tradition on Fashion Street, Andheri West.
